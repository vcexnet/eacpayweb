6、How much EAC is needed to publish announcement type messages?

There are currently four types of EAC announcement type messages.
  1. Trumpet, ≥ 50,000 EAC/article, mainly for the broadcast of ideas, thoughts, affirmations, concepts for the whole world to know.
  2. Deepthroat, ≥ 100,000 EAC/article, mainly for secret exposure, exposing some industry scandals and unknown secrets and promoting positive energy.
  3. Emotion, ≥1000 EAC/article, providing work, life, family, love, workplace and other kinds of spiteful, emotional expression of their true identity that is not easy for others to know.
  4. Treeholes, ≥100EAC/strip, where anyone who wants to talk, but can't pick an audience for specific reasons, can come here, where no one knows your true identity, and where it hurts to talk.
Notes.
  1. Must not be lower than the standard amount, otherwise it will not be displayed
  2. Exceed the criteria in accordance with the amount of the largest to small display, only the first 50 bars
  3. Below the standard amount will not be displayed and will not be refunded

7、How to publish announcement information

  1. Click on the bottom: Publish.
  2. Select: Announcement.
  3. In the send screen that pops up, select the type of announcement to be sent, enter the message and attachments to be sent, and ensure that there is a sufficient number of EACs in the wallet.
  4. Click send and enter pin.
The number of EACs required regarding the type of announcement is as follows.
  1. Trumpet: >50,000 EAC.
  2. Deepthroat: >100,000 EAC.
  3. Emotion: >1000 EAC.
  4. Tree holes: >100 EAC.
The larger the number the higher the display in the same type of announcement, according to the EAC confirmation speed, wait about 1 minute, refresh the page can be seen in the selection of the home page corresponding message type inside.

8、How to post general messages

  1. Click on the bottom: Publish.
  2. Choice: General message.
  3. In the sending pop-up screen, enter the recipient's address, alias, eac number, message, and attachment, respectively.
  4. Click send and enter pin.
Depending on the speed of the EAC confirmation, wait for about 1 minute and refresh the page to see it in "My Messages".

15、What is the private key?

The private key is the proof of your account and is very important, unique, non-resettable and non-recoverable. If you find that your private key is in danger of being exposed, please transfer the EAC in your wallet to another secure EAC address. there are two separate types of private keys in EACTALK.
  1. The EAC helper word, which is the result of making the Earthcoin blockchain private key readable, must be remembered.
  2. IPFS private and public keys, please make a backup (inside the IPFS node settings), copy or write them on paper media for safekeeping, which is the proof of your assets stored on the IPFS network.
